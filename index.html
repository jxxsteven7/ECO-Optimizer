Ecological cycling provides a dynamic search metaphor.

01 / Overview

Search as a living
system of exchange.

ECO views a population as an ecological system rather than a collection of independent search agents. Energy flow directs useful information through the population, while material cycling renews diversity and preserves the capacity to escape local optima.

02 / Method

Three ecological roles,
one coordinated search.

01

Producers

Generate and distribute candidate information, providing broad search directions for population evolution.

Information supply
02

Consumers

Herbivores, carnivores, and omnivores apply distinct predation behaviours to refine and diversify the search.

Adaptive interaction
03

Decomposers

Recycle population information through optimal, local-random, and global-random decomposition mechanisms.

Diversity renewal

The roles are designed to maintain a dynamic balance between global exploration and local exploitation throughout the optimization process.
